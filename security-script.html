Training Notes


Head to the training room teleport with your trainee
Use any open training room


Congratulations on reaching the security division. We are extremely happy to have you.

During this session, you will be learning how to operate our security system.

This system may be complex to some members so please do not hesitate to ask questions.

Our security system is in place to prevent non-authorized personnel from entering HQ.

There will be a short quiz along with a simulator to test your knowledge. Any questions?


(Wait on their reply)


There are 3 sections to this training - Steps, Operation and Search.

We will go over the first section - steps. In this section, you will learn about basic security.

The person willing to enter will be teleported on the rollers at security.
From there

You are supposed to open the gate first using the command :gate. Then
you will check for their correct divisional badge, motto and attire.
Any questions on the security steps?


(Wait on their reply)

We will now move on to security operations. This is a crucial part of the training
So please pay close attention.
There are a total of 2 stations in HQ as you've seen. Please note SV and HR+ members
must use their respective gates. At this station, you will have the following commands.
:green, :blue, :red
If their rank is within the divisions standard, security or training, you must
Verify them through on the portal under “Members” > Security Search> Search
the username.
Once verified for entry, you must double check for attire and badge.
From there, say the command :green inorder to teleport him/her to the base
If their rank is stated as [NSA] Recruit, they are in need of basic training.
Ensure they spelled their motto correctly, and attire/badge is worn.
Correct them if needed, otherwise you may let them through training by saying
:blue
If they have no rank, and they are not in the portal database you are
not to grant them access to base. If they are behaving in a troll behaviour,
you are to give them one warning before using the command :red
Note that this command can also be used on SV/VIP members.
Do you have any questions?

(Wait on their reply)


Our final portion of the training will be based on security search.
This is where portal access is required. If you do not have portal
access please tell me now otherwise we will continue.


(Wait on their reply)

(If they have portal access, skip to 'they have portal access' section. Otherwise, follow the rest of the script)

We will now move on to portal sign up. If you are on mobile, we advise
you to go on a laptop or PC. Otherwise please open a new tab on your browser,
and head to portal.nsahabbo.com . Let me know once there.

(Make sure they correctly go to the site. Help them if needed.)


From there, you will need to create an account. Click on 'Register' and begin
following the steps to creating an account. Let me know once done.

We will now go over everything you have learned in this training.
With this short test, you'll be able to properly operate security.
Any questions before we begin?


NSA Security Test

(Only copy and paste bolded lines!)

Question 1: A civilian on your roller has the [NSA] Special Visitor badge.
What do you do?

Let them know to use the SV gate, and say :red

Question 2: An NSA worker has the correct motto and badge. What do you do?

Verify their entrance status on portal, and say :green

Question 3: The worker on your roller has the correct motto and badge,
but you cannot find them on the portal. What would you do?

Ask for assistance from a higher up member

Question 4: A user is on your roller with the motto {NSA} Recruit.
What must you do?

Help them change their motto by replacing the brackets around 'NSA' with '[ ]',
then verify badge and attire before saying :yellow

Question 5: What are the four commands on roller? Just name them.

The four commands are :gate , :red , :green and :yellow


(They must get 5/8 or higher to pass)


Congratulations! You have successfully learned how to operate Security!

rules.

Security.
If you’re playing Habbo on mobile, we highly recommend you to FTF instead.
Note: You can always go to any worker or help desk if you require assistance.
Please also request the security badge, and favourite it.
Good luck and welcome to the Security Division!
