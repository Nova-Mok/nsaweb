Special Units

Discover the specialized units within the NSA, each bringing unique expertise to elevate operational efficiency. From Legal Affairs ensuring adherence to Finance ensuring precision, these units work cohesively. The Events Unit crafts engaging experiences, while the External Relations Unit navigates external collaborations. Collectively, they embody excellence, integrity, and innovation, propelling the NSA's mission forward.

Transfer unit

The Transfer Unit specializes in managing transfers from external agencies to the NSA.
- Ensuring the quality and efficiency of the transfer process is a primary focus of the unit.
- Dedicated to offering the best experience to capable transferees.
- Committed to providing a warm and welcoming environment for all members joining through transfers.
- The ultimate goal of the Transfer Unit is to optimize the transfer experience for all interested parties.
Events unit

The Events Unit, consisting of dynamic, personable, and multi-skilled individuals, plays a crucial role in coordinating a variety of activities within and outside the NSA base. Their primary duties involve:
- Facilitating and overseeing games and events to enhance engagement and enjoyment.
- Strategically organizing special events during notable occasions.
- The Events Builder specializes in elevating room designs and managing technical aspects for both NSA and external events.
- Collaborating cohesively as a team to curate distinctive and memorable experiences for all participants.
Finance unit

The Department of Finance guarantees accuracy and timeliness in payments
- Thorough eligibility validation processes are implemented.
- Comprehensive monitoring occurs both prior to and following disbursements.
- Compliance standards are strictly adhered to during monitoring.
Foreign Affairs

Foreign Affairs constitutes a specialized division within the NSA, dedicated to handling external affairs. Our core competency involves adeptly managing interactions with external agencies and alliances. This unit is characterized by:
- Strategic focus on external aspects of NSA operations.
- Expertise in navigating and cultivating relationships with outside entities.
- Specialization in coordinating with external agencies and fostering alliances for mutual benefit.
Legal Affairs

The Legal Affairs Unit, an esteemed specialized division within the NSA, is entrusted with a range of critical responsibilities, including:
- Helping resolve staff complaints and managing conflicts within the organization.
- Supporting individuals in filing appeals concerning unfair disciplinaries and cases with other members.
- Offering legal advice on organizational policies and procedures.
- Conducting meticulous investigations into reported incidents, ensuring fairness and impartiality.
- Working closely with other units to guarantee compliance with legal standards and regulations.
