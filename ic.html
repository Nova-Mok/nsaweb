Chain of Commands


The NSA implements a structured command system, spanning from Foundation to Leadership levels. Members occupying these elevated positions are the organization's highest-ranking members, responsible for overseeing operational and administrative tasks. Positioned at the apex of the command hierarchy, Foundation members guide the organization, while Leadership members assume roles at the lower tier. Each level carries unique responsibilities that collectively contribute to the efficient execution of NSA's daily operations.
