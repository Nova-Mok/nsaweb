Recruit Training Script

Red Text: Trainer Note

Black and Bold Text: Copy and Paste into Habbo

Orange Number: Total Points per Question

Blue Text: Answer

Training Notes


Head to the training room teleport with your trainee
Use any open training room


Welcome to the NSA Recruit Training session!

I will be your trainer for today.

Today, you will master the rules and commands of NSA!

Please do not AFK/Idle during this training session, or you will fail your training.

In the end, there will be a test, so I recommend you to take notes.

Are you ready to begin?

(Wait on their reply)


First, let's start with the rules in NSA.

1) Treat everyone with respect regardless of rank, gender or race.

2) You must never ask for promotions or room rights.
3) You must never self-promote or double job by working at another agency
4) You must never AFK at FTF/FTS/FTT.
5) Always listen to commands from your higher-ups.
If you see anyone breaking any NSA rules, report it to a higher rank immediately.
Do you have any questions?

(Wait on their reply)

Next, let's take a look at the commands we use at NSA!
Attention or ATT: Stand up behind your chair, wave and say, ‘Yes Sir or Ma'am’.
At Ease or AE: Sit back down and continue what you were doing.
Fill The Front or FTF: Fill the front desks.
Fill The Back or FTB: Fill the AFK stations.
Back To Base or BTB: Go to the NSA HQ.
Clear The Hallway or CTH: You must clear the hall that you're standing in
Do you have any questions?


NSA Recruit Test

(Only copy and paste bolded lines!)

Question 1: What is FTF?

Fill The Front (1pt)

Question 2: What is FTB?

Fill The Back (1pt)

Question 3: What is BTB?

Back to Base (1pt)

Question 4: What are 2 things to check for when allowing someone into NSA?

To check that the correct badge is pinned on their profile (1pt)

To check that their motto is accurate (1pt)

Next please demostrate these commands.

ATT!

*Note: They should stand up, wave and say ‘Yes Sir/Ma’am’* (1pt)

AE!

*Note: They should sit down* (1pt)

Finally, please tell me 2 NSA Rules!

*Note: Recruit will have to name any of the two rules shown below* (2pt)
a) Treat everyone with respect regardless of rank, gender or race.
b) You must never ask for promotions or room rights.
c) You must never self-promote or double job by working at another Habbo Agency
d) You must never AFK at FTF/FTS/FTT.
e) Always listen to commands from your higher-ups.


*Note: Recruit will need to answer 7/10 correctly in order to pass.*


If they failed:

I regret to inform you that you have failed the training session.
No worries as you can retake the test.
Would you like to retake your test now?

*Note: Retrain Recruits or send them out*


If they passed:


Congratulations, you have passed the NSA Training Session!
Please change your motto to: [NSA] Agent I [Trainer’s Tag]
To enter the base, please make sure you remember my username.
You will need to remember future promoters as well.
That is all, you may now BTB, congratulations again and Welcome to the NSA family!

(Head back to base with your security agent)
