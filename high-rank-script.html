High Rank Script


This is a reminder for the Trainer; you should copy and paste line by line and avoid going
too fast or too slow. You are permitted to use colored chat during the training.

Hello and welcome to the High Ranks Division!
This training will help you through the basics of
what being a High Rank means.
It will cover all HR responsibilities, roles, and duties.
You are expected to understand these rules at all times.
Any questions before we begin?


(Wait for their reply)


Here is what to expect as a High Rank member
You may promote members from now onwards.
You are given the power to issue a verbal warning to the LRs; if they are breaking any rules.
You can now enter the base through HR gate.
Each division has its own promotion time-frame.
You can find more information on this policy on
our web in the Code of Conduct tab.
Do you have any questions?
Before promoting an individual, you should take look of their online time.
Have they been online and filling a station for at least 10 minutes?
We'll now go over promotion policies to ensure that every worker is treated fairly.
1. Promotion should only be given on hard work and eligibility.
2. You cannot promote anyone within the same division as you.
3. You must have been actively working with an online time of 30 minutes before promoting.
4. Promotions must be closed 10 minutes before before pay and may remain after pay.
Note that when you promote some worker(s) to a new division,
you are required to train them accordingly.


Any Questions?


Now we'll learn the basics of Promotion.
Go to our portal menu there you'll see members list.
When you are doing a promotion you should check their online time.
And time between promotions although the portal will show when they can promote.
Call the worker to an attention and allow them to change their motto.
Once they have completed, remember to say 'AE' (At Ease).
Now we'll learn how to log their promotion.
Look for the member list tab and search for the member name who you're promoting.
Then, kindly click on the promote button on the far right
Do you have any questions?
Moving on, you are allowed to warn, strike and demote members
all within certain ranks with a valid reason.
If you are in need to fire a member you must see approval from 5iC+.


Any Questions?


We will now have a short test.
NSA High Rank's Test
Q1: Are you allowed to promote anyone in the division as you?
(no)
Q2: How long must a worker be online to earn a promotion?
(30 minutes)
Q3: How do you log a promotion?
(going on portal and click promote next to the worker's name)
4: Name the 3 Divisional trainings HR+ can conduct.
(Recruit, Security and training)
(They must get 4/6 or higher to pass)

Congratulations on passing the test!
If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to ask me!
You may also dress however you'd like from now onwards, cheers!
Please request and favorite the HR badge.
Before we BTB, I would like to inform you that you are now allowed to join Special Units.
You can find forms in our discord server.
Do you have any questions about special units?
We wish you the best of luck in your new division!
BTB
