Promotion Policy

Requirements and general regulations

1. Only member ranked HR+ and above can promote.
2. Promotion jurisdiction is one division below the member’s current division. Members may not promote someone within their own division.
3. The Foundation and CEO is the authority in charge of promoting members ranked 5iC and above. Foundation Reps may carry out iCloud promotions only by the order of the foundation.
4. Any Founder may overrule any promotion policy at their discretion
5. The minimum time while in base needed for a person to conduct a promotion is 10min. The promoter must be on the working floor. Being in the FTB or AFK chairs does not count towards those 10 minutes.
6. The minimum time while filling a station needed for a person to be promoted is 10minutes
7. If someone notices any violation in the rules above they are required to inform the promoter. If the promoter does not cancel the promotion they are to submit the complaint form with the valid proof.
8. The maximum amount of promotions in a row to the same person is 2 unless there is nobody else.
9. When promoting someone to Security, Trainer they must get trained as per NSA training program
10. No promotions may be given 15 minutes before pay starts.
11. If someone disconnects they can log the proof in #dc-proof channel on discord and get a vouch.
12. All promotions must meet these requirements and be logged on the portal, if not the promotion is declined
NSA Code of conduct

General Rules and Procedures

1. Every member of the agency is expected to respect one another regardless of rank, age, race, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religion, color, or disability. All members must follow the Habbo way. These expectations also apply to behavior external to NSA, such as when visiting other agencies.
2. Colored chat is not permitted in base unless you are ranked Leadership (5iC) and above, a VIP, or making an announcement permitted by Foundation.
3. Visitors may not use colored chat unless VIP, as stated above.
4. ICs and VIPs may use any colored chat excluding all neon and the pirate theme.
5. Wearing obscene or offensive outfits is prohibited within the NSA rooms and whilst representing NSA externally.
6. Asking for pay, or rights is prohibited.
7. Members should refrain from requesting promotions unless their promotion is well-overdue and they have been actively working at a station to earn it. If a member feels that they are eligible for a promotion, they can raise this concern at the help desk only. Those assisting on the help desk may honor the promotion at their discretion.
8. The use of effects is permitted throughout the base.
9. Dancing is only permitted in the lobby and events area.
10. NSA members should ensure that they are active and reactive when filling a station in base. If idling or inactivity becomes too frequent, this may result in disciplinary action.
11. NSA members who hold room rights, at their discretion, can remove inactive members from the station if they fail to respond within 10 seconds when called to attention. These members will be sent to FTB using the furni bop.
12. Profile and online time visibility must be turned on at all times.
13. Uniforms must be worn at all times. Exceptions are made for those who have reached a certain rank. There will also be uniform free weekends.
14. Rank list can be found here: nsahabbo.com (Rank List)
15. All official communications must happen under official servers with the presence of members of Foundation. Exceptions to this rule can be made when giving feedback, private conversations or interviews.
16. Accessing or registering another users Portal account is prohibited without prior consent from a member of iC's.
